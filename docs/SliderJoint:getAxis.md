SliderJoint:getAxis
===

Get the axis of the slider.

    x, y, z = slider:getAxis()

### Arguments

None

### Returns

- `number x` The x component of the axis vector.
- `number y` The y component of the axis vector.
- `number z` The z component of the axis vector.
