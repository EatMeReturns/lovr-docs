Transform:scale
===

Scale the Transform.

    transform:scale(x, y, z)

### Arguments

- `number x` The amount to scale the Transform along the x axis.
- `number y` The amount to scale the Transform along the y axis.
- `number z` The amount to scale the Transform along the z axis.

### Returns

Nothing
