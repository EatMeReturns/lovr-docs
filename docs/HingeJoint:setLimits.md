HingeJoint:setLimits
===

Set the upper and lower limits of the hinge angle.  These should be between -π and π.

    hinge:setLimits(lower, upper)

### Arguments

- `number lower` The lower limit.
- `number upper` The upper limit.

### Returns

Nothing
