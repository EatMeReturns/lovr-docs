BallJoint:setAnchor
===

Sets the joint anchor point.

    ball:setAnchor(x, y, z)

### Arguments

- `number x` The x position of the anchor.
- `number y` The y position of the anchor.
- `number z` The z position of the anchor.

### Returns

Nothing
