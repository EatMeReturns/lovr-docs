Shape:getPosition
===

Get the position of the shape, relative to the collider it's attached to.

    x, y, z = shape:getPosition()

### Arguments

None

### Returns

- `number x` The shape's x offset.
- `number y` The shape's y offset.
- `number z` The shape's z offset.
